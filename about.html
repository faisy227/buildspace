Who We Are

Founded in 2010, BuildSpace has grown from a small studio to an internationally recognized architecture firm with projects spanning across three continents.

Crafting Spaces That Tell Stories

Founded in 2010 by Michael Chen, BuildSpace began as a small studio with big ambitions. Today, we’re a global firm with offices in San Francisco, New York, London, and Tokyo, but we’ve never lost our passion for thoughtful, human-centered design.

Our approach combines cutting-edge technology with time-honored craftsmanship. We believe buildings should not only serve their purpose but elevate the human experience, connect with their surroundings, and stand as testaments to our clients’ visions.

Each project begins with deep listening – to our clients, to the site, to the community. This dialogue informs every decision, from material selection to spatial organization, resulting in designs that feel both inevitable and surprising.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to international recognition, our story is one of growth, innovation, and unwavering commitment to design excellence.

2010

Founding of BuildSpace

Michael Chen establishes BuildSpace in a small San Francisco studio with a vision to create architecture that bridges tradition and innovation.

2012

First Major Award

Our Cliffside Residence project wins the AIA Design Excellence Award, marking our emergence as a firm to watch.

2015

New York Office Opens

We expand to the East Coast with our Manhattan office, allowing us to take on larger-scale urban projects.

2017

Sustainability Initiative

We establish our dedicated sustainability practice, committing to carbon-neutral designs across all projects.

2020

Global Recognition

BuildSpace is named one of the "World's Most Innovative Architecture Firms" by Architectural Digest.

2023

Tokyo Expansion

Our Asia-Pacific office opens in Tokyo, bringing our design philosophy to new cultural contexts.
