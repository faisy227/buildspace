Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our architectural services.

What's included in your basic architectural services?

Our basic architectural service package includes initial consultation, schematic design, design development, and construction documents. We'll work with you to develop the initial concept, refine the design, and produce the necessary technical drawings for permitting and construction.

How long does a typical architectural project take?

Project timelines vary significantly based on scope and complexity. A small residential project might take 3-6 months for design and permitting, while a large commercial development could take 12-24 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline tailored to your specific project.

What makes BuildSpace different from other architecture firms?

Our integrated approach combines innovative design with practical construction knowledge. We emphasize sustainability without compromising aesthetics, and our full-service model means you'll have consistent support from initial concept through project completion. Our clients particularly appreciate our transparent communication and commitment to bringing their vision to life.

Do you work with clients outside your local area?

Yes, we regularly work with clients nationally and internationally. With modern collaboration tools, we can effectively manage projects remotely while coordinating with local teams as needed. We're licensed in multiple states and can obtain additional licensure as required for your project.

How do you handle project budgets?

We begin each project with a thorough discussion of your budget parameters. Throughout the design process, we provide cost estimates and work to align the design with your financial goals. Our team has extensive experience with value engineering to maximize quality while maintaining budget discipline.
